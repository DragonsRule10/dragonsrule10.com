Hey there, I'm Tristin, also known as Dragon (DragonsRule10) online, and I've been diving into the coding world since 2021. Coding has always been a significant part of my life, and I've had some cool experiences along the way.

I've been actively involved in Minecraft development, particularly with Dragons Studio. We worked on some awesome projects, including plugins and mods that added a whole new dimension to the Minecraft experience. It's incredible how coding can transform a game, and being a part of that process was truly rewarding. My coding journey also took me into the realm of Discord development. I was part of the Warden project under Dragon Studio, where I got hands-on with Python to create Discord bots. It was a bit different from Minecraft, but I loved the challenge and the chance to explore a new avenue of coding. Another exciting chapter was my time with Society Unites. Society Unites was my first ever Minecraft Development Position. Here, I wore multiple hats – from crafting Minecraft plugins to diving into HTML and CSS, and even taking on server moderation and management tasks. It was a well-rounded experience that allowed me to apply my coding and moderation skills in various ways. I'm proud of my proficiency in HTML and CSS, and my journey in Python has been pretty exciting too. Currently, I'm diving into the world of Java, eager to add another language to my coding arsenal.
Being at a young age and already immersed in the coding world feels pretty surreal, but I'm loving every moment of it. There's always something new to learn, and I'm excited to see where my coding journey takes me next.
